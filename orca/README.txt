The current directory contains templates for the 
Orca Quantum Chemistry program

1.water_sp_dft: Water ground state: single point (SP) calculation (B3LYP)
               - basis set: aug-cc-pVQZ (#Contracted BF: 2*46+80 = 172)
               - method: B3LYP
               - freq. calculation

2.water_sp_cc: Water ground state: single point (SP) calcualtion (CCSD(T))
               - basis set: aug-cc-pVQZ (#Contracted BF: 2*46+80 = 172)
               - method: CCSD(T)
               - freq. calculation

3.methane_opt_cc: Methane ground state: geometry optimization (CCSD(T))
               - basis set: aug-cc-pVTZ (#Contracted BF: 4*23 + 46 = 138)
               - method: CCSD(T)
               - freq. calculation

4.co2_opt_cc: CO2 ground state: geometry optimization (CCSD(T))
               - basis set: aug-cc-pVTZ (#Contracted BF: 3*46 = 138)
               - method: CCSD(T)
               - freq. calculation
